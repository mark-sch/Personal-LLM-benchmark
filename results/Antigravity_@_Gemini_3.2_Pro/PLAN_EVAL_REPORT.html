Stakeholder Readout

This is a promising architectural start, not a ready-to-execute parity plan. Core infrastructure and feature shells are in place, but readiness is constrained by behavior-level specificity and compliance guardrails.

Best covered: stack, schema framing, route scaffolding Needs tightening: lifecycle + AI contracts Highest risk: secrets/compliance + migration continuity

Decision signal: proceed only with a revision pass focused on the top critical gaps, then re-run full coverage evaluation.
