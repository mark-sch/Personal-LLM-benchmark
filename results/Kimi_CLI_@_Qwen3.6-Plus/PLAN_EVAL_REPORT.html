Coverage Narrative

Overall Posture

A structurally strong plan with thorough infrastructure, data model, and UI architecture coverage. All 30 critical requirements fully covered. Gaps concentrate in the AI quality and behavioral nuance layer — not in the foundation.

Strength Clusters

Infrastructure & Isolation (17/17), Show Data Model & Persistence (saving triggers, merge logic, timestamps), Show Detail UX (14/14) with full narrative hierarchy, and Navigation Shell with clear routes for all three discover modes.

Weakness Clusters

~65% of partials cluster in AI quality & behavioral contracts. The plan treats AI as an infrastructure concern (provider abstraction, structured output) but under-specifies product-level behavioral expectations: concept quality filtering, ordering, surprise, and quality gates.

Remediation Guidance

The gaps require surface-specific behavioral specification, not architectural redesign: AI quality gate definitions, concept system quality specs, response format contracts per surface, and settings persistence detail for local/UI state keys.

Top Gaps — What Breaks Without These

▲ Critical-to-Fix

  • PRD-091 — Discovery Quality Rubric important
    Without rubric scoring and hard-fail integrity checks, there is no mechanism to catch hallucinated recommendations, generic concepts, or AI voice drift before they reach users.
  • PRD-084 — Surprise in Recommendations important
    Without deliberate surprise + defensibility criteria, the system risks producing only safe, genre-adjacent picks — undermining the product's core differentiator of taste-aware discovery.
  • PRD-077 — Concept Ordering important
    Without ordering by strength and axis diversity, concept chips become flat and repetitive, causing users to disengage from the alchemy flow.

▼ Should-Fix

  • PRD-066 — Direct Answer Format important
    Without structuring for direct answers within 3–5 lines and confident stance, Ask responses can become verbose and hedge-y, burying the recommendation and reducing user trust.
  • PRD-073 — Structured Output Retry important
    Without retry-once-then-fallback for malformed showList parsing, a single AI output format slip breaks the entire mentioned-shows strip UI, degrading the Ask experience.
